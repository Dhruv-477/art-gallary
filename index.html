Featured Artists

Leonardo da Vinci

Mona Lisa, oil painting on a poplar wood panel by Leonardo da Vinci, probably the world's most famous painting. The sitter's mysterious smile and her unproven identity have made the work a source of ongoing investigation and fascination.

Ravi Varma

Ravi Varma was born into an aristocratic family of Kerala. He taught himself the intricacies of oil painting and the practices of European naturalism. His paintings of Indian deities and mythological scenes are among his most celebrated works.
